# SkillSwap

Because sometimes, friends explain it better.

---

## What is SkillSwap?

SkillSwap is a peer-to-peer learning platform where students **teach what they know and learn what they need**.

Instead of one-way learning, it creates a system where:

* a student good at DSA can help someone struggling with it
* and in return, learn something like design, music, or public speaking

It’s built around a simple idea — learning becomes easier when it’s **relatable, collaborative, and at your own pace**.

---

## What the Product Does

SkillSwap matches students based on:

* skills they can teach
* skills they want to learn
* availability
* urgency

Once matched, users can:

* connect with peers
* generate and edit a shared timetable
* track sessions and progress
* give feedback and ratings

The goal is to make it feel less like a platform, and more like a **learning network among students**.

---

## Tech

* **Frontend:** React, custom CSS, Context API
* **Backend:** Node.js, Express, SQLite

---

## Why this matters

Most students don’t struggle because content is unavailable —
they struggle because it’s not explained in a way they understand.

SkillSwap tries to solve that.
